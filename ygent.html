YG Entertainment Profile

A premium, modern, and responsive company profile website for a leading global entertainment agency.

❮ ❯

About Project

The YG Entertainment Profile website is a frontend development project focused on creating a high-end digital experience for fans and stakeholders. The goal was to modernize the company's web presence, focusing on artist showcase, news distribution, and global branding. Built as a static yet highly interactive web application, it ensures fast loading times and seamless responsive behavior across all devices.

🎯 Problem

Representing a global powerhouse like YG Entertainment requires a digital presence that feels as high-end as their talent. Traditional static sites often feel outdated and fail to capture the dynamic energy of the entertainment industry.

💡 Solution

Developed a sleek, modern frontend using clean HTML/CSS and smooth JavaScript interactions. Focused on a minimalist yet impact-heavy design that puts the artists and content at the forefront.

Key Features

🏠

Intuitive Home Page

A flagship entry point featuring dynamic highlights and a clean hierarchy of information.

🎤

Artist Showcase

Dedicated artist sections designed to highlight visual identity and recent works of the talent roster.

📢

Notice & News Center

A structured and aesthetic way to distribute official announcements and company updates.

👥

About & Audition

Informative pages that facilitate talent discovery and provide deep insights into the company mission.
